About us

Game developers design and create video games. They work in teams those they include: actors, programmers, producers and marketing specialists.
Game developers mainly specialize in a specific game platforms (eg PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Mobile: IOS or Android), and special in directions such as: programming, artificial intelligence, gameplay etc.
In some cases, when creating games, Game Developers combine several jobs at the same time.

Why Choose Game Development?

Maybe because it's a career that involves cutting-edge technology and a combination of creativity. It can also be because you love play video games and want to bring your creative ideas to life. Maybe you? Do you want a career that allows you to travel? during or from home. Maybe you like drawing and making things on your own? manually through a virtual platform. There are many reasons you will want to work in game development, and there are dozens of career paths that will open up to you in many fields.
